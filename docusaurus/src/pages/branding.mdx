## Logo

Wechaty has two types of logo:

1. Rectangle Logo
1. Square Logo

with two colors:

1. Green
1. White

They are all SVG format with transparent background.

### Rectangle Logo (Green)

[![Wechaty](../../static/img/wechaty-logo.svg)](https://github.com/wechaty)

- Markdown

